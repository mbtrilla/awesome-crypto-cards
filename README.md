# Awesome Crypto Cards [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of crypto debit & credit cards.

Crypto cards let you spend cryptocurrency (or stablecoins) at any merchant accepting Visa or Mastercard. This list tracks every major product across custodial exchanges, self-custody wallets, and neobank integrations. Data is maintained at [Sweepbase](https://sweepbase.net), a free aggregator comparing 139+ cards by fees, cashback, custody model, and regional availability.
